Pizza Capriciosa Recipe

Capricciosa Pizza is a classic Italian pizza. The "Capricciosa", as everybody calls it, is on every pizzeria's menu, usually under the list of traditional pizzas. Its staple ingredients are tomato puree, mozzarella, cremini mushrooms, artichoke hearts, black olives, and prosciutto cotto (Italian cooked ham).

Ingredients

  • crushed canned plum tomatoes
  • mozarella
  • prosciutoo cotto
  • artichoke heart wedges in olive olive
  • fres cremini mushrooms
  • pitted black olives

How to Pizza Capriciosa step-by-step

  1. Knead flour and water until you get a soft dough, then knead in first the yeast, and then the salt and the olive oil.
  2. Let the dough rest for 10 minutes, then turn it over on a table and knead it for a few minutes.
  3. Place the dough in an oiled bowl which you then need to close with a lid or cover with a plastic wrap. Refrigerate for 24 hours.
  4. The following day, on a floured surface, stretch the dough to the desired shape, taking care that you leave a raised 2 cm (0.8-inch) rim on the outer edge of the dough.
  5. Then, spread the tomato sauce over the dough, and bake it in a 250°C/480°F oven for 10 minutes.
  6. Take the pizza from the oven and top it with chopped mozzarella, ham, mushrooms, and artichokes, then bake for another 10 minutes.
  7. Before serving, drizzle the pizza with olive oil.
